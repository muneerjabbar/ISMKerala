March 15-17, 2024 Calicut, Kerala

Youth Leadership Summit 2024

A comprehensive three-day program designed to develop leadership skills among young Muslims. The summit will feature workshops, seminars, and interactive sessions led by renowned Islamic scholars and community leaders. Participants will learn about effective leadership principles, community service, and how to make a positive impact in society.

Program Features

  • Interactive workshops on leadership development
  • Sessions on Islamic values and modern challenges
  • Networking opportunities with like-minded youth
  • Community service projects
  • Cultural programs and entertainment
