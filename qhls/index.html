Quran Hadith Learning School (QHLS)

Comprehensive Islamic education program for students and youth

About QHLS

The Quran Hadith Learning School (QHLS) is one of ISM Kerala's flagship programs designed to provide comprehensive Islamic education to students and young people. Our program combines traditional Islamic learning with modern educational methods to create an engaging and effective learning environment.

Program Objectives

  • To provide authentic Islamic knowledge based on Quran and Hadith
  • To develop proper understanding of Islamic principles and values
  • To encourage critical thinking and analytical skills
  • To foster love for learning and knowledge
  • To prepare students for responsible citizenship

Course Structure

Quran Studies

  • Quranic Arabic
  • Tajweed (Proper Recitation)
  • Memorization (Hifz)
  • Tafseer (Interpretation)

Hadith Studies

  • Introduction to Hadith
  • Major Hadith Collections
  • Hadith Methodology
  • Practical Application

Islamic Jurisprudence

  • Fiqh Fundamentals
  • Worship (Ibadah)
  • Social Ethics
  • Contemporary Issues

Islamic History

  • Prophet's Biography
  • Rightly Guided Caliphs
  • Islamic Civilization
  • Muslim Contributions
