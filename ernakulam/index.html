About ISM

ISM Ernakulam is District wing of ISM, Kerala which is youth organization of Kerala Nadvathul Mujahideen, dedicated to nurturing young Muslim hearts through comprehensive programs that blend spiritual growth, educational excellence, and social responsibility. ISM educate and enable muslims to follow pure Islam and fight against Shirk and Bida'ths.Our organization operates across 16 districts of Kerala as well as Other major indian cities and GCC countries, providing guidance and support to students and youth in their spiritual journey.

Our Activities

Quran Hadith Learning School (QHLS)

A well-structured curriculum designed to teach the Qur’an and Hadith through a specially crafted syllabus and examination system, implemented across hundreds of centers in Kerala and beyond — nurturing a generation grounded in authentic Islamic knowledge.

Read More

Velicham

Velicham is a structured, recurring Qur'an study initiative led by the ISM Kerala State Committee. With the goal of bringing the divine message of the Qur'an closer to people's lives, Velicham empowers individuals to reflect, understand, and act upon the timeless guidance of the Qur'an in a meaningful way.

Read More

Dawah

Dawah is at the heart of ISM’s mission — spreading the eternal message of Islam to people everywhere. Through individual and collective outreach initiatives, ISM inspires the youth to embody Islamic values and share them within society. With a thoughtful and compassionate approach, the Dawah activities aim to convey the truth, mercy, and peace of Islam to all humanity.

Read More

Eelaf

Eelaf focuses on nurturing unity, brotherhood, and social harmony within the community. It serves as ISM’s platform for building meaningful human connections rooted in Islamic values. Through welfare programs, social outreach, and community development activities, Eelaf strives to create a compassionate and cohesive society inspired by the teachings of the Qur’an and Sunnah.

Read More

Vichinthanam

Vichinthanam, a platform that nurtures discernment and awareness, is the official weekly publication of ISM. Its aim is to deeply observe subjects such as religion, society, culture, and education, to awaken the thinking of the younger generation, and to guide them towards a correct Islamic perspective. This publication, which opens avenues for reflection and discussion, serves as a heritage platform that strengthens ISM’s mission both spiritually and intellectually.

Read More

Inspire

Dive into a transformative journey of knowledge and spirituality with ISM Inspire! this specially crafted series of interactive Islamic sessions is dedicated to cultivating a scholarly audience among professionals, both within and beyond the borders of Kerala. Our primary mission is To elevate religious awareness and upskill the spiritual journey of professionals like you.
