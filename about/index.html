About ISM Kerala

Nurturing young Muslim minds through comprehensive programs

Our Mission

ISM Kerala is the youth wing of Kerala Nadvathul Mujahideen, dedicated to nurturing young Muslim minds through comprehensive programs that blend spiritual growth, educational excellence, and social responsibility. We believe in empowering the youth to become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society while maintaining their Islamic values and principles.

Our Vision

To create a generation of educated, spiritually aware, and socially responsible Muslim youth who will lead the community towards progress while upholding Islamic values and contributing to the betterment of society at large.

Our History

Founded in the early 1990s, ISM Kerala has been at the forefront of Islamic student movements in Kerala. Over the years, we have grown from a small group of dedicated students to a statewide organization with presence in all 16 districts of Kerala. Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, innovative programs, and unwavering commitment to the cause of Islamic education and community development.
