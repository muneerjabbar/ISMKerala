About ISM Kerala

ISM Kerala is the youth wing of Kerala Nadvathul Mujahideen, dedicated to nurturing young Muslim minds through comprehensive programs that blend spiritual growth, educational excellence, and social responsibility. Our organization operates across 16 districts of Kerala, providing guidance and support to students and youth in their academic and spiritual journey.
