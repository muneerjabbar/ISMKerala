About Velicham

Velicham (Light) is our educational enlightenment program designed to illuminate young minds with knowledge, critical thinking, and intellectual curiosity. Through various educational initiatives, we aim to foster a love for learning and develop well-rounded individuals who can contribute meaningfully to society.

Program Features

Study Circles

Regular study sessions on various topics

Seminars & Workshops

Educational seminars and skill development workshops

Peer Learning

Collaborative learning among students

Competitions

Educational competitions and quizzes
